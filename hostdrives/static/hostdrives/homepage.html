Our Mission

Inspire and empower individuals to take action towards keeping our streets, neighborhoods, and public spaces clean and litter-free. Our goal is to create a culture of cleanliness and civic responsibility in our city. We believe that by working together, we can make a lasting impact and create a better future for our community.

We are a community-driven initiative that aims to make our society a cleaner and more livable place for everyone. At UJJWAL, we believe that everyone has a role to play in keeping our city clean. We invite you to join us in our mission. Whether you want to volunteer for a cleanup event, organize a litter-free campaign in your neighborhood, or simply spread the word about our cause, there are many ways to get involved and make a difference.

About Us

Features

People can register themselves on the website and organize cleanliness drives in their areas, providing service to the community. People can volunteer at cleanliness drives through the website by browsing and filtering through the ongoing drives. We provide a connection between people willing to organize cleanliness drives and people who want to volunteer in such drives.
